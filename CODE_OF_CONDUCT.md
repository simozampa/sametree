# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Giving and accepting constructive technical feedback.
- Focusing discussion on observable behavior and engineering tradeoffs.
- Respecting differing experience levels, identities, and viewpoints.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and repairing their impact.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, and publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

For ordinary moderation, open an issue without including sensitive personal details. For a confidential report, use [GitHub private reporting](https://github.com/simozampa/sametree/security/advisories/new) and begin the title with `Conduct report`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this standard. They may temporarily or permanently ban participants for behavior that is harmful to the project or its community.

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.
